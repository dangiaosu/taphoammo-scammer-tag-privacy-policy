Privacy Policy for TapHoaMMO Scammer Tag

Last Updated: April 6, 2025

Thank you for using TapHoaMMO Scammer Tag ("the Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency.

1. Data We Collect

The Extension does not collect any personal or sensitive user data. It performs the following actions:

  • Website Data: The Extension accesses the DOM (Document Object Model) of web pages on taphoammo.net to identify seller names and tag potential scammers. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.
  • Scammer List: The Extension fetches a list of scammers from third-party Google Apps Script APIs. This list is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API (`chrome.storage.local`).

2. How We Use Your Data

The data accessed by the Extension is used solely to provide its core functionality: identifying and tagging potential scammers on taphoammo.net. Specifically:

  • DOM data is used to identify seller names and append a "SCAMMER" tag with a reason where applicable.
  • The scammer list is used to match seller names against known scammers.

All processing is done locally on your device, and no data is shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

The scammer list fetched from the API is stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`. This data is automatically updated every 2 hours to ensure accuracy. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the Extension or clearing your browser's storage.
